Sponsorship

Sponsorship at the Space Impact Forum enables leaders across business, policy, and research to pioneer solutions that strengthen our planet’s resilience. By supporting the Food, Energy, Environment, and Urban Impact Labs, sponsors help accelerate practical innovation, informed policy, and measurable progress across global sustainability challenges.

  1. Home
  2. Sponsorship

Why Sponsor the Space Impact Forum?

The Forum brings together leading organisations to co-create solutions for some of the world’s most urgent system-level challenges. Sponsoring SIF is not just brand visibility — it’s influence, collaboration, and impact.

Shape Policy & Industry Direction

Contribute to agendas that inform regulation, investment, and global sustainability pathways.

Connect with Global Decision-Makers

Engage leaders from government, academia, industry, and the broader innovation ecosystem.

Support Cross-Sector Innovation

Help build practical solutions to food security, energy transition, environmental health, and urban resilience.

Support the Impact Labs

The four Labs form the core of the Space Impact Forum’s work — each tackling a critical global system with space-enabled and cross-sector solutions.

Food Impact Lab

Advancing food security, resilience, soils, crops and supply chain sustainability through data-driven insights and innovation.

Energy Impact Lab

Accelerating the transition to clean, reliable, and equitable energy systems with scalable interventions.

Environment Impact Lab

Strengthening planetary health through monitoring, restoration, biodiversity, pollution tracking, and climate resilience.

Urban Impact Lab

Improving urban resilience, infrastructure, planning, mobility, and liveability through data and systems design.

Sponsorship Packages

Flexible partnerships to match organisational ambitions — from sector leadership to research acceleration.

Strategic Partner

Lead sponsor tier for organisations driving global transformation agendas.

  • Brand leadership across the entire Forum
  • Influence in Lab direction-setting
  • Priority speaking opportunities
  • Custom research or policy development options
Impact Lab Sponsor

Support a specific Lab aligned with organisational purpose or sector focus.

  • Branding within a chosen Lab
  • Participation in workshops & co-design sessions
  • Inclusion in Lab publications
  • Access to Lab insights, prototypes, and outputs
Supporting Partner

Diversified support across Forum programming, community, and events.

  • Brand presence across core events
  • Participation in roundtables and networking
  • Access to sponsor-only reports & briefings
  • Optional involvement in Lab-specific streams

Commitment to Impact & Transparency

Every sponsorship directly supports evidence-based research, measurement, and reporting. The Forum uses Theory of Change, LogFrames, and a unified Impact Framework to track progress and ensure clarity, accountability, and real-world outcomes.

Clear Metrics

All Labs use defined indicators for social, environmental, and economic outcomes.

Regular Reporting

Sponsors receive exclusive updates, dashboards, and impact summaries.

Evidence-Based Outputs

All interventions are backed by research, tested prototypes, and cross-sector validation.
